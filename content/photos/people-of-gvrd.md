I walk around Greater Vancouver and ask random strangers for permission to
photograph them. There's no real motive — just to force myself to engage more
with my surroundings, talk to people, and hopefully share something
interesting.

If you're one of the people I met on these walks: thank you for being open to
it, and for being part of this little experiment. If you have any objection to
the use of your picture, [email me](mailto:shiv@shiv.me) and I'll sort it out.
