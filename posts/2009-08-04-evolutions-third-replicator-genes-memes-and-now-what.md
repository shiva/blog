> The first replicator was the [gene](http://www.newscientist.com/topic/genetics) - the basis of biological [evolution](http://www.newscientist.com/topic/evolution). The second was memes - the basis of cultural evolution. I believe that what we are now seeing, in a vast technological explosion, is the birth of a third evolutionary process. We are Earth's Pandoran species, yet we are blissfully oblivious to what we have let out of the box.</p>
