The reason for the respite in my blogging, was my move to a new web-host. After several hours of researching, I decided to go with [webfaction.com](http://www.webfaction.com/?affiliate=shvelmur). If you haven't already visited webfaction, [DO IT right now](http://www.webfaction.com/?affiliate=shvelmur)! It has been simply amazing. I love the one click installer, and within hours of getting billed, I got access to my account. It comes with shell access, SFTP, enough space, [rails 2.x](http://www.rubyonrails.org/) (just days after it was release), [django](http://www.djangoproject.com/), [wordpress 2.3.1](http://wordpress.org/download/) and a host of other webapps, all of which you can install with a just a few clicks on their web interface.
