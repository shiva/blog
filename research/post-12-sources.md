## Storyteller Tactic
**Data Detectives** — Zoom out: what is temporal reasoning? Zoom in: the concrete anomaly (OpenAI Memory's 21.71%). Detective question: why does the world's best model fail this? The clue: timestamps. The conclusion: two different problems masquerading as one.

## Key Point
Temporal validity (enforced by the memory system) and temporal reasoning (performed by the model) must both be present — and getting one right does not substitute for the other.
