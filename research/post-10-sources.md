## Storyteller Tactic
**Good & Evil** — not a simple good vs evil: two rights in conflict. GDPR protects individuals (care vs harm). EU AI Act protects public trust in AI (authority vs subversion). Both are legitimate. The conflict is architectural, not moral — and the architecture has to solve it.

## Key Point
The EU AI Act and GDPR impose directly contradictory requirements on agent memory, and the only architectural pattern that satisfies both is one that no existing framework provides out of the box.
